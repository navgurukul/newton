## Question 6

1.If speed if less than 70, print 70.
2.If speed is more than 70, give 1 point per 5km.(NOTE:This won't count 70).
3. If points are more than 12, print “License suspended”

`Input:-` 


85
135
 
**Output :-**

3
“License suspended”
