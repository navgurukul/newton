### Reading Material

Kisi bi number **x** ka **Factorial** hoga **1 * 2 * 3 * .... * x**.
Jese **5** ka factorial hoga **1*2*3*4*5=120**.

**Factorial** ka sign `!` denote hota hai.

### Question
Apko ek value dijayegi x jiska apko factorial nikalna hai.
